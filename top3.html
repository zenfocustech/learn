Top Cyber Defenses for Small Organizations

The business landscape has changed: cyber attack activity and risk are much higher, smaller organizations are now targets, and most business plans were never budgeted or designed for this problem.

Learn Apply Adjust

Defenses you can do yourself for free

  1. 1) Use 2FA everywhere you can

    Like Nike says, “Just do it.”

    • Best: hardware key like YubiKey 5 — https://www.yubico.com/
    • Free: smart phone app like Authy — https://www.authy.com/

  2. 2) Use a password manager

    Not your browser’s password save function.

    • Free and good: KeePassXC — https://keepassxc.org/
    • Good with group features: Bitwarden — https://bitwarden.com/

  3. 3) Use a Zero-Trust mindset

    Especially with banking, attachments, and credentials.

    • Staff training, policies, and practice!
    • https://www.cybersecurity-insiders.com/zero-trust-is-a-mindset-not-a-milestone-what-business-leaders-need-to-know/
